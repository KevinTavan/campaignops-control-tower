# Scoring Methodology

CampaignOps Control Tower scores launch readiness from 0 to 100.

## Score categories

| Category | Max points |
|---|---:|
| Objective clarity | 15 |
| Audience clarity | 15 |
| Message clarity | 20 |
| Channel coverage | 10 |
| Asset readiness | 15 |
| Stakeholder clarity | 10 |
| Measurement clarity | 10 |
| Timeline clarity | 5 |

## Interpretation

| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 80 to 100 | Strong launch readiness |
| 60 to 79 | Moderate readiness with coordination risk |
| Below 60 | High launch risk |

## Important note

The score is a workflow signal, not a final approval. A high score does not guarantee campaign quality. It only means the campaign inputs are more complete and structured.
