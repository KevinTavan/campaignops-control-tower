# Problem Statement

Integrated marketing teams often work across campaign briefs, launch plans, stakeholder updates, asset trackers, channel calendars, and performance goals. These inputs are usually spread across documents and teams.

## Core problem

Campaigns can look ready in a deck while still having hidden operational gaps.

Common issues include:

- Unclear audience ownership
- Repeated or overlapping messages
- Missing launch assets
- Weak measurement plans
- Unclear stakeholder handoff
- Channel conflicts
- Late discovery of blockers

## Proposed workflow

CampaignOps Control Tower structures campaign inputs into a clear operating brief, readiness score, risk view, and handoff summary.

## Success criteria

A useful campaign operations workflow should:

1. Surface gaps before launch
2. Make ownership clearer
3. Reduce duplicated work
4. Improve campaign handoff
5. Help managers see risk quickly
6. Support, not replace, strategic judgment
